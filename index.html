Project

MidMinder

MidMinder is a mobile app I created during the Technovation Girls competition. It’s designed to help diabetic patients and people with chronic illnesses manage their medication schedules effectively. The app sends reminders, keeps track of pills, and even offers health advice for those who can’t afford frequent medical visits.

I'm deeply passionate about scientific projects they’re where my curiosity meets creativity. Working on MidMinder sparked something in me, and I’m already planning to bring more ideas to life. I believe in using tech not just to solve problems, but to support and uplift people. There’s so much more I want to build, and this is just the beginning.
